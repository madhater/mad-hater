Logo Design

A logo created for a frontend development framework I work on in my free time. I originally started with the name as a sort of counterpoint to the “cloud”. I tried numerous approaches, at one time giving ligatures a shot since I'd never really created one before. During that process I realized one of my attempts looked a lot like a dragon, and that dragon's are creatures of the sky (and awesome). As a piece of technology I used a geometric font in addition to trying to keep the dragon ligature very mathematical. I also created some smaller versions where the detail is reduced so they hold up better at those sizes.
